Seller Psychology
The 5 Seller Archetypes
Not every motivated seller is the same. Six years of cold calls, county record pulls, and closed conversations produced this framework. Each archetype gets a different script, a different opening, and a different structure.
🟢
The Retiring Operator
Owned 20+ years. Wants income, not a tax bill. Loves seller financing.
🟡

Tried to sell 3+ times. Energy gone. Responds to speed and simplicity.
🔴
The Failed Speculator
Investment thesis didn't perform. Underwater. Wants out clean without a loss narrative.
🔵
The Portfolio Exit
Liquidating multiple assets. Wants repeatable, simple closes. Ideal for volume.
⚫
The Distressed Owner
Liens, code violations, financial pressure. Responds to certainty and no contingencies.
Retiring Operator — Full Script Logic

Opening: "You've built something real here. I'm not here to lowball you — I'm here to structure an exit that pays you every month for the next 20 years."

The offer: Asking price (or close to it). 10% down. Seller note at 6%. 25-year amortization.

Tax angle: Walk them through the installment sale math. Capital gains tax paid over 25 years vs. one check in April. For long-time operators, this is the conversation closer.

Owner Finance — The Math

Deal structure template:

Purchase price: $X
Down payment: 10–20%
Seller note: 80–90% of PP
Rate: 5–7% (below bank rates)
Amortization: 20–30 years
Balloon: 5–10 years
Monthly payment: DSCR verified > 1.0

Monthly NOI must exceed monthly debt service. We verify this in every LOI before delivery.

05 — Intelligence Layer
Prediction Market Intelligence
The Intelligence Layer
Most buyers look at the current market. We look at what the market will be in 90 days. Prediction market data gives us forward-looking economic signals that most CRE investors don't know exist — and that institutional shops pay $50K+/year to access.
Kalshi
Regulated Exchange
100%
FOMC prediction accuracy since 2022
Federally regulated financial exchange. Traders bet real money on Fed rate decisions. When Kalshi shows 65%+ probability of a rate cut, cap rates are about to compress and property values will rise. We use this to time outreach and frame urgency.
Polymarket
Macro Signals
40.1%
Lower CPI forecast error vs. economists
Decentralized prediction market for macro and policy events. Provides crowd-sourced probability on inflation, unemployment, and policy changes. More accurate than traditional economist forecasts. Gives us the macro context before entering any market.
Polymarket-Parcl
City-Level RE
6–18mo
Lead time over REIT price movements
City-level real estate price prediction markets. Before we approach a seller in Denver, Phoenix, or any target market, we know what direction prices are moving and whether urgency framing is warranted. City-specific intelligence, not national averages.
How We Use This Data
Deal Timing: When Kalshi shows rate cut probability > 65%, we accelerate outreach. Property values are about to rise — the seller's window to sell at current prices is closing.
Seller Psychology: Rate cut probability becomes urgency framing in our scripts: "The rate environment is shifting. Buyers who couldn't finance this 90 days ago will be competing next quarter."
LOI Pricing: Polymarket-Parcl city indices inform whether to offer asking price or negotiate. Rising market = tighter offer. Declining market = negotiate harder.
