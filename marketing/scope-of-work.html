Every opportunity scored across DOM weight, price-to-market ratio, seller-motivation language, financing flexibility, property fundamentals, and cap rate. Only top scores advance.

Five archetype system: Retiring Operator, Failed Speculator, Exhausted Holder, Portfolio Exit, Distressed Owner. Full pain map including personal motivations, negotiation posture, and optimal outreach approach.
