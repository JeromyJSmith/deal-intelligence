1. Services

Provider agrees to perform commercial real estate deal sourcing, analysis, seller profiling, outreach intelligence, and related services as described in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") or Deal Package confirmations ("Order") incorporated herein by reference. Each SOW or Order will specify the scope, deliverables, timeline, and compensation applicable to that engagement.

1.1 Standard Services

Services may include: deal identification and scoring; seller psychological profiling; outreach script development; stakeholder contact research; Letter of Intent preparation; public records analysis; macro economic signal analysis; and deal structure engineering.

1.2 No Guarantee of Outcome

Provider does not guarantee that any identified deal will result in a successful acquisition, that any seller will respond to outreach, or that any deal structure will be accepted by a seller. Provider's obligation is to deliver high-quality intelligence and tools — execution outcomes depend on Client action and third-party responses beyond Provider's control.

1.3 Not Real Estate Brokerage

Provider is not a licensed real estate broker or agent. Services constitute research, analysis, and strategic consulting — not brokerage services. Client is solely responsible for compliance with applicable real estate licensing laws in their jurisdiction.

2. Compensation
2.1 Fees

Client agrees to pay Provider the fees specified in the applicable SOW or Order. Fee structures may include: per-deal package fees, monthly retainer fees, or equity participation in closed acquisitions, as agreed in writing.

2.2 Payment Terms

Unless otherwise specified: (a) per-deal packages require 50% payment upon Order confirmation and 50% upon delivery; (b) monthly retainers are due on the 1st of each month; (c) equity arrangements are documented in a separate written deal memo prior to execution.

2.3 Late Payment

Payments not received within 7 days of the due date accrue interest at 1.5% per month. Provider reserves the right to suspend services for accounts more than 14 days past due.

2.4 Expenses

Pre-approved expenses (database access fees, filing fees, third-party data costs) are reimbursable at cost with receipt documentation.

3. Confidentiality
3.1 Mutual Confidentiality

Each party agrees to hold in strict confidence all non-public information received from the other party, including but not limited to: deal files, scoring methodology, buy box parameters, seller identities, outreach scripts, business strategies, financial information, and system architecture ("Confidential Information").

3.2 Exceptions

Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was rightfully known by the receiving party prior to disclosure; or (c) is required to be disclosed by law or court order (with prompt written notice to the other party).

3.3 Duration

Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

4. Intellectual Property
4.1 Provider IP

All scoring methodologies, psychological profiling frameworks, system architecture, software, and proprietary processes remain the exclusive property of Provider. No license to these underlying methods is granted by this Agreement.

4.2 Deliverable License

Upon receipt of full payment, Provider grants Client a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the specific deliverables provided (deal cards, scripts, LOIs, contact trees) solely for Client's own acquisition activities on the specific property identified. Deliverables may not be resold, sublicensed, or distributed without prior written consent.

4.3 Client Data

Client retains ownership of all data and information Client provides to Provider. Provider may use such data solely to perform services under this Agreement.

5. Term & Termination
5.1 Term

This Agreement is effective as of the Effective Date and continues until terminated by either party.

5.2 Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 14 days written notice. Client remains obligated for all fees incurred for services delivered through the termination date.

5.3 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach by the other party if such breach is not cured within 7 days of written notice specifying the breach.

5.4 Survival

Sections 3 (Confidentiality), 4 (Intellectual Property), 6 (Limitation of Liability), and 7 (Governing Law) survive termination.
