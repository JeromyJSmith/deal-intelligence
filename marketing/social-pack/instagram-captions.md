## CAPTION 05 — Owner Finance Education
**Visual:** Split graphic: "BANK SAYS NO" vs. "SELLER SAYS YES"

When the bank says no, 90% of buyers disappear.

We see that as a competitive advantage.

Banks refuse to lend on special purpose properties, operating businesses, sub-$1M deals, and anything "non-standard."

That wall removes almost every competitor.

Owner financing bypasses the bank entirely. The seller becomes the lender. The structure is negotiated between buyer and seller — not dictated by an underwriting committee.

Below bank rates. No appraisal. No DSCR committee. Close in weeks.

The "unlendable" deal is often the best deal.
