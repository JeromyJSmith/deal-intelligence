## What We Post About (Content Pillars)

### 1. THE DATA (40% of content)
Facts, figures, and intelligence that educate the audience on the opportunity.
- "437 average days on market across our 8 active deals. That's not a warning sign. That's leverage."
- "Owner-financed properties close at 6–8% seller note rates. That's lower than current bank rates. The seller is the bank."
- Prediction market signals and what they mean for CRE timing

### 2. THE SYSTEM (30% of content)
How it works — in ways that create curiosity, not full disclosure.
- "3,000 listings → 8 signals → 4 qualified deals. Every week, automatically."
- Show the filter, not the formula
- Highlight the intelligence layer (prediction markets, seller psychology)

### 3. THE DEALS (20% of content)
Real deal examples, blurred/redacted for IP protection. The deal card visual is our hero asset.
- "Score: 95/100. 404 days on market. Confirmed seller financing. Denver, CO."
- Never reveal full details publicly — curiosity is the CTA

### 4. THE THESIS (10% of content)
Big picture thinking about the market moment and why this approach wins now.
- Interest rate environment → why sellers can't sell to conventional buyers
- Why banks refusing to lend on special-purpose properties is actually good for us
- The intelligence gap between institutional and individual buyers

## Voice & Tone Rules

**DO:**
- Be specific with numbers (437 DOM, not "long days on market")
- Lead with the insight, not the pitch
- Sound like you know something other people don't — because you do
- Use short sentences for punchy moments, longer ones for context
- Let the data do the selling

**DON'T:**
- Use the words "guru", "secret", "hack", "crush it"
- Mention CREXI by name (that's our source, not public knowledge)
- Say "AI" without context — say "multi-agent pipeline" or "intelligence system"
- Promise returns or guarantees
- Post anything that sounds like a course sale or coaching pitch
