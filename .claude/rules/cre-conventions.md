# Commercial Real Estate Conventions

## Financial Metrics

- **Cap Rate** = NOI / Purchase Price — express as decimal (0.08 not 8%)
- **NOI** = Gross Income - Operating Expenses (exclude debt service)
- **GRM** = Purchase Price / Gross Annual Rent
- **DSCR** = NOI / Annual Debt Service (>1.25 is healthy)
- **Cash-on-Cash** = Annual Pre-Tax Cash Flow / Total Cash Invested
- **Price Per Unit** = Purchase Price / Number of Units

## Property Type Terminology

| We Say | CREXI Category | Notes |
|--------|---------------|-------|
| MHP | Mobile Home Park | Also "manufactured housing community" |
| NNN | Retail (Triple Net) | Tenant pays taxes, insurance, maintenance |
| C-Store | Convenience Store | Often paired with gas station |
| Value-add | Any underperforming asset | Below-market rents, deferred maintenance, vacancy |
| Stabilized | Fully occupied, market rents | Lower risk, lower cap rate |

## Owner Financing Terms

- **Seller carry** = Owner provides the financing (same as "owner financing")
- **Down payment** = Buyer's initial equity contribution
- **Note** = The promissory note documenting the loan terms
- **Deed of trust** = Security instrument recorded against the property
- **Balloon payment** = Remaining balance due at a specified future date
- **Amortization** = Period over which payments are calculated
- **Interest rate** = Annual rate charged on outstanding balance

## Standard Deal Structure (Owner Financed)

```
Purchase Price: $X
Down Payment: 10-20% ($X * 0.10-0.20)
Seller Note: 80-90% of purchase price
Interest Rate: 5-8% (negotiable)
Amortization: 20-30 years
Balloon: 5-10 years
Monthly Payment: Amortized over chosen period
```
