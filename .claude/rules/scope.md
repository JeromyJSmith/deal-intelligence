# Deal Intelligence Scope — NOT CRE-Limited

## The Rule

Deal Intelligence is NOT commercial real estate software.
Deal Intelligence is NOT limited to CREXI.
Deal Intelligence is NOT defined by any single asset class.

## What It Actually Is

Deal Intelligence is a **motivated-seller acquisition intelligence system**.
The methodology applies to ANY deal, ANY asset class, ANY market:

- Real estate (residential, commercial, land, special purpose)
- Businesses for sale
- Notes and mortgages
- Franchises
- Mobile home parks, storage, car washes, gas stations
- ANY asset with a motivated seller

## The Intelligence Layer Is Universal

- Motivated seller signals (DOM, price cuts, language patterns)
- Prediction market intelligence (Kalshi, Polymarket — macro signals)
- Behavioral psychology (seller profiling, negotiation science)
- Automated deal scoring
- Owner financing structure
- Zero bank dependency

These tools work the same whether the asset is a duplex, a laundromat,
a business, or a note. The platform is the methodology, not the asset class.

## Enforcement

- NEVER describe Deal Intelligence as "a commercial real estate system"
- NEVER limit scope to CREXI or CRE platforms
- NEVER assume that a deal must be real estate
- ALWAYS frame as: acquisition intelligence for motivated-seller deals
- ALWAYS include "any asset class" when describing scope

## Copy Standard

When writing about Deal Intelligence, use:
- "acquisition intelligence system" (not "real estate system")
- "cash-flowing assets" (not "properties")
- "any asset class" (not "commercial real estate")
- "motivated-seller opportunities" (not "CRE listings")
- "deal intelligence" (not "property intelligence")
