## Days on Market Thresholds

| DOM Range | Interpretation |
|-----------|---------------|
| 0-30 | Fresh listing, likely firm on price |
| 31-90 | Normal marketing period |
| 91-120 | Starting to age, seller may negotiate |
| 121-150 | Motivated — open to creative terms |
| 151-180 | Highly motivated — owner financing likely |
| 180+ | Desperate — strongest negotiating position |

## Red Flags

- Expense ratio below 25% for any property type (understated expenses)
- Cap rate above 15% (verify income is real and sustainable)
- NOI increasing while occupancy is declining
- "Pro forma" financials only, no actuals provided
- Listing agent is also the owner (potential conflict)
- Recently renovated but selling quickly (flip or hidden issues)
