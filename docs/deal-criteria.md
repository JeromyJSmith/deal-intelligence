# Deal Criteria

## Target Profile

Properties that can be acquired with owner financing (no or low money down)
that generate immediate cash flow.

## Ideal Deal Characteristics

- **Owner financing available** (seller carries the note)
- **Cash flow positive** from day one
- **Motivated seller** (high DOM, price reductions, retirement, estate)
- **Essential service** property type (housing, fuel, storage)
- **Below-market pricing** relative to comparables
- **Cap rate > 8%** (higher = better for buyer)
- **Value-add potential** (below-market rents, vacancies to fill, deferred maintenance)

## Target Property Types (Priority Order)

1. **Mobile Home Parks** — Low management, steady cash flow, affordable housing demand
2. **Self Storage** — Recession-resistant, low maintenance, scalable
3. **Gas Stations** — Essential service, captive C-store revenue
4. **Car Washes** — Simple operations, recurring revenue
5. **Multifamily (small)** — Cash flow, appreciation, tax benefits
6. **Motels** — Deep value-add if distressed

## The Strategy (From the Playbook)

1. Go to CREXI.com
2. Search for owner-financed listings OR listings with high days-on-market
3. Target properties listed 5+ months ago that haven't sold
4. Call the seller/broker and propose: "I'd like to make payments to you
   at your asking price"
5. Focus on sellers with specific pain:
   - Retiring (wants income stream, not tax hit)
   - Estate/heirs (wants simple exit)
   - Tired landlord (wants relief from management)
   - Failed to sell (exhausted conventional buyers)

## Deal Structure Template

```
Purchase Price:  [Asking price or negotiated]
Down Payment:    0-20% (negotiate lowest possible)
Seller Note:     80-100% of purchase price
Interest Rate:   5-7% (win-win: seller earns more than savings, buyer gets below-bank rate)
Term:            20-30 year amortization
Balloon:         5-10 years (refinance or sell before balloon)
Monthly Payment: Calculated from above
```

## Quick Math Check

Before pursuing any deal, verify:
- Monthly NOI > Monthly debt service payment (DSCR > 1.0)
- Annual cash flow after debt service > $0
- Cap rate > interest rate on seller note (positive leverage)

## Disqualifiers

- Cap rate < 5% (unless premium market with appreciation play)
- NOI cannot be verified or is "pro forma" only
- Environmental issues (gas station contamination, etc.)
- Litigation pending against property
- Seller unwilling to provide any financing
- Property in severe physical decline beyond repair budget
