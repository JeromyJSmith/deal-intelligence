The Origin Story
Six Years to Get Here
I Didn't Set Out to Build a System.
I Set Out to Buy a Gas Station.

I didn't set out to build a real estate technology company. I set out to acquire a cash-flowing property with no money down — specifically, an owner-financed commercial asset where the seller carries the note. That first deal taught me two things that would define the next six years:

First: The intelligence gap between sophisticated buyers and sellers is massive. Sellers with properties sitting unsold for 300+ days are sitting on negotiable deals — they just need the right buyer to frame the right offer. Most buyers never show up because they don't know where to look or how to read the signals.

Second: Almost all of that intelligence is publicly available. County assessor records. State SOS filings. Days-on-market history. Broker license records. Seller psychology patterns embedded in listing language. The raw material exists — what was missing was the system to aggregate and weaponize it.

"Every project after that first deal became an experiment. What could I systematize? What could I automate? What could I teach a machine to see that I was spending hours finding manually?"

— The core question that drove six years of iteration

Today, Deal Intelligence is that system. It runs a full acquisition pipeline — from raw platform search to a pre-populated Letter of Intent — for any target market, any property type, in under 24 hours. And every deal it closes feeds pattern data back into the system, making the next search faster and the next offer sharper.

The Build Timeline
Yr 1–2
Manual Discovery Built spreadsheets. Learned to read DOM data as a seller motivation signal. Developed the first cold-call framework for 180+ day listings. Success rate improved with each attempt.
Yr 3–4
Templating & Tools Systematized the research process. Built reusable templates for county assessor lookup, SOS entity research, broker profiling, and seller psychology mapping. Each tool saved hours per deal.
Yr 5–6
AI & Automation AI changed everything. Multi-agent pipelines replaced manual research. The system now analyzes listings, profiles sellers, generates scripts, maps contact trees, and pre-populates LOIs — automatically.
Now
Deal Intelligence v1.0 Fully operational. 8 active deal files across 3 markets. Complete outreach infrastructure per deal. This briefing is the first formal stakeholder presentation of the system in production.

The Compounding Principle: Every deal closed generates pattern data. Every pattern improves the next search. The system gets smarter with each acquisition — this is a compounding intelligence advantage, not a one-time tool.

How the System Works
The Five-Phase Pipeline
From Raw Platform Data to
a Signed Letter of Intent.
1
Discovery — Market Scan Multi-platform search for properties with long days-on-market (180+ days), seller financing signals, and target property types: gas stations, car washes, mobile home parks, flex industrial, restaurants, and multifamily. The system surfaces what motivated sellers look like before anyone calls.
2
Deal Scoring — 100-Point Analysis Every listing is scored on: DOM weight, price-to-market ratio, financing term signals, seller motivation language, property type quality, and market conditions. Scores rank deals by acquisition priority — the highest scores are the fastest paths to a close.
3
Seller Profiling — Psychological Mapping We identify the seller's archetype: Retiring Operator, Failed Speculator, Portfolio Exit, Distressed Owner, Completely Exhausted. Each archetype has a distinct pain profile, negotiation posture, and optimal outreach approach. This is the intelligence layer that most buyers never access.
4
Outreach Intelligence — Multi-Channel Scripts For each deal: a 3-call close sequence (calibrated to seller archetype), email templates, LinkedIn notes, a full stakeholder contact tree (Tier 1 broker → Tier 2 brokerage → Tier 3 seller direct), and a 21-day outreach cadence across all channels. The system knows what to say, to whom, and when.
5
Contractual Framework — LOI Ready Pre-populated Letter of Intent for each deal: purchase price, down payment, seller note amount, interest rate, amortization, balloon term, monthly payment, and earnest money deposit. Every number is calculated. The deal is ready to put in writing before the first call.
What Makes This Different

The Stealth Principle. Every outreach script is calibrated to the seller's exact pain points — but never reveals that we know them. We show up as a humble buyer who happened to find the listing. The intelligence stays internal. The conversation stays disarming.

No Bank Required. Every deal in this system is structured for owner financing. The seller carries the note. The buyer makes monthly payments. No bank approval, no appraisal delays, no conventional financing walls. The deal math works because both sides win.

Scope Without Creep. Traditional research means weeks of manual work. Automation means the full pipeline — discovery through LOI — runs in under 24 hours per market. Scope is no longer a constraint. We analyze everything because we can.
