# Cold Outreach — New Contacts / Strangers

Use these when reaching out to people you DON'T know — real estate investors, syndicators, wholesalers, or anyone in CRE who might pay for leads or partner up.

The goal: get them curious enough to take a call WITHOUT giving away how you find deals.

---

## TEXT — Cold (From Social Media, Networking Event, Referral)

> Hey [Name], [how you found them — "saw your post about X" / "we connected at the RE meetup" / "[Mutual friend] mentioned you"]. Quick question — are you or your investors actively acquiring commercial properties? I've got access to a deal pipeline that might be worth a conversation. No pitch, just seeing if there's a fit.

### Variation — LinkedIn Message

> Hi [Name] — came across your profile and it looks like we're in similar lanes. I've built a proprietary system for sourcing owner-financed commercial deals across all 50 states. Not looking to sell anything, just exploring whether there's potential to collaborate. Open to a quick call?

### Variation — After Seeing Their Content

> Hey [Name], been following your content on [platform] and I respect what you're building. I've got something that might complement what you do — a pipeline that identifies motivated sellers offering owner financing on cash-flowing commercial assets. Worth 10 minutes?

## EMAIL — Cold Introduction

I've built a data-driven system for identifying commercial properties and operating businesses where the seller has already signaled willingness to provide owner financing. These aren't cold leads or skip-traced contacts — these are active, public listings where the seller's motivation and financing flexibility are already visible.

Here's why I think it could be relevant to you:

- **Scale**: 1,400+ opportunities identified across 45+ states in the initial scan alone
- **Variety**: Mobile home parks, storage, gas stations, restaurants, retail, hospitality, industrial
- **Qualification**: Every deal is scored on seller motivation, financing flexibility, and economics

I'm not a guru selling a course. I'm a deal sourcer who built something that works and I'm looking for the right people to work with — whether that's providing leads on a fee basis, partnering on acquisitions, or something else entirely.

If you're actively acquiring or have investors who are, I'd love a quick intro call. 10-15 minutes, no obligation.

Best,
Jero
[Phone / Email]

## DNC (DO NOT DO)

- **Don't mention CREXI by name** — that's your source, not theirs
- **Don't explain the scoring system** — that's your IP
- **Don't send actual listings** — they'll just go find them themselves
- **Don't use "AI" or "algorithm"** — say "system" or "method" — people tune out buzzwords
- **Don't apologize for reaching out** — you're offering value, not asking for favors
- **Don't send long messages** — curiosity beats information in cold outreach
