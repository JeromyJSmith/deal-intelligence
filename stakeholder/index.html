That first deal — owner-financed, motivated seller, zero bank involvement — taught me everything I needed to know about where the real opportunity lives. Every project after that became a laboratory. What could I systematize? What could I automate? What took me 40 hours to research could be done in 40 minutes — if the right tools existed. So I built them.
